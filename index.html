Welcome to Sree Krishna Residency

Nestled just 300 meters from the renowned Guruvayur Temple, Sree Krishna Residency offers a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and spiritual serenity. Our premium hotel is the ideal choice for pilgrims, families, and travelers.

With 62 well-appointed AC and Non-AC rooms, modern amenities, and warm Kerala hospitality, we ensure every guest enjoys a comfortable experience.
