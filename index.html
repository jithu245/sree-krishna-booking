About Guruvayur Temple

Guruvayur Temple is one of the most famous and revered Hindu temples in Kerala, dedicated to Lord Krishna. Known as the "Dwarka of the South", this temple attracts millions of devotees every year.

The temple is managed by the Guruvayur Devaswom Board under the Government of Kerala. The deity here is worshipped as Lord Vishnu in his form as Lord Krishna.

4th richest temple in Kerala
500+ years of history
Millions of devotees annually
Traditional Kerala architecture

Legend

According to legends, the idol was installed by Lord Krishna's devotees after the destruction of Dwarka. The name "Guruvayur" comes from "Guru" (Lord Brihaspati) and "Vayur" (wind).
